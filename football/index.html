Introduction

Steve and Nick are lifelong Man City fans, going to a few matches a season. When it was announced that City had signed up for the Super League (in 2021), we were... disappointed. We remain City fans. However, we decided that when we wanted to watch a match, we would also consider matches at our regional clubs.

Our goal was to incorporate the elements we enjoyed when we went to see a City match. We would go by public transport, find a good chippy nearest to the ground and, with a nod to the modern age, have a veg or vegan pie at half time, as well as a pint.

Our first trip was to Hallam FC, which is a 10 minute walk from where we live, missing out the chippy and pie.
